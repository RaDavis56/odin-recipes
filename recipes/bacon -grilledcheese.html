Bacon Grilled Cheese

Savory Cheese and Crispy Bacon Grilled Cheese

Smoky, crispy bacon meets gooey, melted cheese in this indulgent twist on the classic grilled cheese sandwich. Sandwiched between two slices of golden, buttery bread, the salty crunch of bacon perfectly balances the creamy richness of the cheese. Whether you're making a quick lunch or craving a late night comfort meal, this bacon grilled cheese delivers bold flavor and irresistible texture in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread (whatever type you perfer)
  • Bacon Strips, thick cut
  • Sliced Cheese, (once again whichever you like, but cheedar or america is recommended)
  • Softened Butter
  • Optional: Avocado or parsley

Directions

  1. Cook the Bacon

    In a skillet over medium heat, cook bacon strips until crispy, about 6–8 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess grease.

  2. Prep the Bread

    Spread softened butter (or mayo, if preferred) on one side of each slice of bread

  3. Assemble the Sandwich

    Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, on a skillet or pan.

    Layer with 1–2 slices of cheese, 2–3 strips of cooked bacon, and an additional slice of cheese for extra meltiness.

    Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.

  4. Grill the Sandwich

    Heat a skillet over medium-low heat. Cook the sandwich for about 3–4 minutes per side, pressing gently with a spatula, until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is fully melted.

  5. Serve Hot

    Slice and enjoy immediately optionally with tomato soup, a pickle, or your favorite dipping sauce.
