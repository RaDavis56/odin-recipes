Mozzarella Cheese Sticks

Melt in Your Mouth Cripsy Cheese Sticks

Golden, crunchy, and irresistibly gooey these homemade mozzarella cheese sticks are everything a cheese lover dreams of. Each stick is coated in a perfectly seasoned breadcrumb crust, then fried to crispy perfection, revealing a melty, stretchy mozzarella center with every bite. Served hot with a side of marinara or your favorite dipping sauce, they make the ultimate appetizer, snack, or party crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • MOzzarella Cheese Sticks. Either string cheese or block cut
  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Italian-style breadcrumbs
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Dried Oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Vegatable oil
  • Marinara Sauce (optional)

Directions

  1. Prepare the Cheese:

    Cut mozzarella into sticks about 3 to 4 inches long if not using pre-cut string cheese. Place in the freezer for 30–60 minutes to help prevent melting during frying.

  2. Set up the breading Station

    In one bowl, place about 1/2 cup of flour.

    In a second bowl, whisk 2 eggs with 2 tablespoons of milk.

    In a third bowl, mix equal parts Italian-style breadcrumbs and panko (about 1 cup total), then season with 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/2 teaspoon onion powder, 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.

  3. Bread the Cheese Sticks

    Dip each frozen cheese stick first in flour, then in the egg mixture, and finally coat thoroughly in the breadcrumb mix.

    For extra crispiness, repeat the egg and breadcrumb steps for a double coating

  4. Freeze Again, (optional)

    Place breaded sticks back in the freezer for 20–30 minutes. This helps them keep their shape and prevents leaking while frying

  5. Frying Process

    First heat the oil. Pour vegetable oil into a deep pan or pot to about 2 inches deep. Heat to 350°F (175°C).

    Fry in small batches for about 1 to 2 minutes, or until golden brown. Do not overcrowd the pan

    Then you want to start draining and cooling. Remove with a slotted spoon and place on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

  6. Serve Hot

    Enjoy immediately with warm marinara sauce or your favorite dip
